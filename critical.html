Why Critical Minerals Matter

July 15, 2025

Critical minerals are the backbone of today’s advanced energy and technology systems. From powering electric vehicles to enabling renewable energy grids, minerals like cobalt, lithium, and neodymium are essential for innovation and sustainability.

However, dependence on a handful of countries for these resources presents risks for energy independence and security. That’s why exploring and securing diverse sources for these minerals is vital for a resilient future.
